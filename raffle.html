About Charity Week

Charity Week for Orphans and Needy Children is:
  • A non-profit organisation
  • 100% volunteer led (no one gets paid!)
  • Raises money for needy children and orphans one week a year
  • Acts according to Islamic principles
  • Does not discriminate against any volunteer, donor or recipient
  • Is not a charity, but instead operates through its association and partnership with a host charity Islamic Relief
